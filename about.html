My Story

Meet the Artisan

"I believe in the beauty of things that take time. When you carry a piece by Asha, you are not just carrying a bag — you are carrying hours of love, undivided attention, patience and heart."

Hi, I am Asha — the sole maker behind every single piece you see here. What started as a quiet evening ritual slowly grew into a deep passion and a calling. Crochet found me at a time when I needed it most, and now it is my life's work.

I make every item myself, with my own two hands. I do not outsource. I do not run assembly lines. Each product is born from hours of careful, meditative work — and that is something you can feel when you hold a piece.

Custom Orders

Custom work is my absolute favorite. If you have a vision — a specific color, a shape, a size, a feeling you want to capture — reach out. I will listen carefully and create something that is entirely, beautifully yours.
