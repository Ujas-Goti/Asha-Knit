Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Asha's Knit ships handmade products across India. Please read this policy before ordering.

Shipping coverage

We currently ship within India only.

Order processing

Orders are usually processed within 2–5 business days after payment confirmation. Custom or made-to-order items may need the full processing window or longer depending on design and materials; we will confirm the timeline when you order.

Delivery time

After dispatch, estimated delivery is 3–7 business days within India, depending on your location. These are estimates, not guarantees.

Courier partner

We ship through Delhivery and may use other reliable courier partners when needed.

Shipping charges

Shipping charges, if any, will be shown at checkout or confirmed before your order is finalised.

Tracking

When your order is shipped, we will share tracking details by email, WhatsApp, or SMS where possible so you can follow your package on the courier’s website.

Delivery issues

Please provide a complete and correct shipping address and phone number. We are not responsible for failed or delayed delivery caused by incorrect details you provide.

If your order is delayed, appears lost, or is marked delivered but not received, contact us and we will help coordinate with the courier.
