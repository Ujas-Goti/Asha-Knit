Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At Asha's Knit, every product is handmade with care. Please read this policy before placing an order.

Returns

We do not accept returns on our products.

Refunds

We do not offer refunds on our products.

Damaged or defective items

If your order arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 10 days of delivery with your order details and clear photos of the product and packaging. After we verify the issue, we will offer a replacement at no extra product cost. We will bear the shipping cost for sending the replacement.

This applies only to damage or defects confirmed on delivery. It does not cover normal handmade variations, change of mind, or damage after delivery due to misuse or improper care.

Cancellation — ready-made orders

You may cancel a ready-made order only before it is dispatched. To cancel, contact us as soon as possible with your order details.

Cancellation — custom orders

Custom and made-to-order items cannot be cancelled once the order is placed and confirmed. Production may begin shortly after confirmation.
